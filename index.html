The Photographer

Traveling the world,
one frame at a time

I'm Ryan Muratore, a photographer based in New York City. I travel as much as I can and bring everything I see back home through my photos.

I'm drawn to the small stuff — the things you'd walk right past. I shoot landscapes and wildlife exactly as they are, so people who weren't there can feel like they were right alongside me.
